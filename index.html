About Hydrobit

Hydrobit is a developing software platform for managing, importing, analysing, and visualising hydrogeological and environmental datasets.

Designed for

Groundwater modelling workflows
Hydrochemistry
Multivariate statistics
Data analytics
Pumping test analysis
Quantitative hydrogeology
